The creators of Hogwarts are four of the greatest witches and wizards that ever lived . Their names were Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff and Salazar Slytherin.
In Hogwarts there are 4 houses based on the creators last names and the four houses are Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff and Slytherin. These houses have their own sybols that have their own meaning, the symbol of Gryffindor was a lion and the students in this house were very brave next is Ravenclaw their symbol was a eagle and the students of Ravenclaw were the cleverest of all now it's Hufflepuff their symbol was a badger and they valued hardwork and at last comes Slytherin and their symbol was a snake and the students of Slytherin are indeed cunning.
